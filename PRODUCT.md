# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Relocating Professionals & Families who need to compare cities on key life domains (cost of living, safety, public services, etc.) to make high-stakes moving decisions. They need clear, unbiased, comparison-ready data that they can trust, as relocating involves high financial and personal commitment.

## Product Purpose
Geoscout is an AI-powered city research and relocation comparison tool. It generates deep, scored insights across 7 core domains so users can assess and contrast potential destination cities before relocating. Success means a user can quickly digest a city's report, understand its trade-offs relative to their current city or criteria, and confidently narrow down their relocation choices.

## Brand Personality
Analytical, Expert, Authoritative. The voice is objective, precise, and data-backed. Tone is professional but accessible. Emotional goals: instill confidence, reduce decision fatigue, and provide a sense of structure and clarity.

## Anti-references
- **Generic SaaS Template**: No oversized pastel glow blobs, decorative illustration cards, or high-contrast card grids with non-functional icons.
- **Dense Database Dump**: Avoid raw spreadsheets, unformatted text blocks, or tables that lack visual hierarchy and semantic structure.
- **Hard Terminal Vibe**: Do not use neon colors, harsh monospace-only layouts, or retro cyber aesthetics that feel niche rather than expert.

## Design Principles
- **Expert Density**: Design for rich, scannable data layouts. Information density should convey thoroughness without causing cognitive overload.
- **Contextual Comparison**: Ground unfamiliar scores and metrics in relative comparison (e.g., comparing cost-of-living index to the user's current location).
- **Typographic Authority**: Rely on stark, intentional typography, font weight, and structured spacing rather than decorative shapes or border-stripes to establish order.
- **Honest Trade-offs**: Give pros, cons, and recommendations equal visual weight. Do not bury negative points or elevate scores artificially.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **WCAG AA Compliance**: Standard AA contrast (minimum 4.5:1 for body and placeholder text) against dark background.
- **Keyboard & Focus**: Clear visual focus rings for all interactive elements, supporting full keyboard navigation.
- **Semantic Structure**: Proper markdown element translation (using header tags, lists, blockquotes) to ensure assistive technology can navigate reports.
